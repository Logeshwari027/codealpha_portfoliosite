01 — Profile

About Me

"Chemistry is the central science — I am learning to speak its language fluently."

I am Logeshwari E, a dedicated B.Tech Chemical Engineering student at KPR Institute of Engineering and Technology, Coimbatore. I have a deep fascination with the science of transforming substances — from molecular theory to full-scale industrial processes.

My academic journey has exposed me to thermodynamics, mass transfer, process control, reaction engineering, and fluid mechanics. Beyond textbooks, I enjoy applying these principles through laboratory experiments, mini-projects, and real-world case studies.

I am passionate about the intersection of chemical engineering and sustainable development — exploring how cleaner processes, green chemistry, and energy-efficient systems can address the environmental challenges of our era.

Outside the laboratory, I engage with workshops, paper presentations, and technical fests that sharpen my problem-solving mindset and connect me with a broader community of innovators and engineers.
